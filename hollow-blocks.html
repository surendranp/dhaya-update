Dhaya Hollow Blocks

Dhaya Hollow blocks offer a speedier, cost effective, and environmentally sound alternative to conventional walling materials.

As we all know, construction is much faster with hollow blocks. Hollow blocks can be used in both load bearing and non-load bearing applications.

Dhaya concrete blocks are used in low and high rise buildings, for basements, exterior and interior walls and partitions.

APPLICATIONS

  • Multifunctional Complexes
  • Institutional Building

  • Residential Complexes

  • Schools, Colleges, Hospitals

  • Factory Buildings & warehouses
