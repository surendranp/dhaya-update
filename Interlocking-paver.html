Intorlocking Paver Blocks (Lacquered)

Intorlocking Paver Blocks (Regular)

Dhaya Intorlocking Paver Blocks create fascinating landscapes on available outdoor space which will enhance the building’s visual appeal thereby increasing the overall value of your property. We offer a wide range of paver shapes and color combinations.

Dhaya Pavers - a favorite with Engineers offer classically elegant and aesthetically pleasing pattern designs, timeless in their appearance and create a beautiful durable and virtually maintenance-free surface for a wide array of paving projects that practically ‘brings the inside out’.

Some characteristics of our paving systems are:-

  • The ability to handle a wide range of wheel and concentrated loads.
  • The ability to handle sub-grade movement without failure of the pavement surface.
  • The pavement gains stiffness with use and is ultimately unaffected by increase in load.
  • If localized settlement occurs it can be easily repaired with almost complete reuse of the original pavers.
  • Ease of access to underground services.
  • Construction methods which use readily available equipment and generally semi-skilled labour.
  • Immediate access for traffic on completion.
  • Colored pavers can be used for locating underground services or indicate special use areas such as crosswalks.
  • The surface has good durability and skid resistance.

APPLICATIONS

  • Residential, Driveways
  • Walkways, Parking lots
  • Shopping Malls
  • Residential Streets
  • Public Parking
  • Bus Stops

Drycast Concrete Paver Blocks
