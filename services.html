About Us

Dhaya Industries is a major company engaged in the manufacturing of wide range of construction products such as heavy duty drycast paver blocks, table made Interlocking paver blocks, hollow concrete blocks, kerb stones and fly ash bricks. All products are manufactured using high grade & quality raw material with latest generation world class automatic block making machines by dedicated teams of experts.
